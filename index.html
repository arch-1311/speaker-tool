TEDx Speaker Evaluation Tool

Toggle the switches for each question:

CONTACT WITH THE TEDx TEAM
The person is a friend or a relative of a team member
The person is recommended by a team member who is a "big fan"
The person uses the dedicated form to nominate him/herself to talk at your event
The person does not mention any idea in his/her application
The person contacts a team member directly on Social Media, instead of using the dedicated form to apply
The person applies several times or contacts your team several times to know if you will consider his/her application
You receive more than 5 nominations for this person (unless it is Simon Sinek)
HISTORY WITH TEDx & OTHER CONFERENCES
The person already did a TEDx within the last 24 months
The person already participated in more than 2 TEDx within the last 48 months
The person participated in more than 10 conferences within the last 24 months
PERSONAL AGENDA & CAUSE BACKING
The person defends a controversial point of view or adheres to a reactionary cause
The person talks at divisive gatherings (extreme political meetings, radical activism rallies, pseudo-scientific conferences...)
The person contributes to dogmatic publications (polarized medias, pseudo-scientific sites or magazines...)
EXPERTISE & AUTHORITY
The person presents him/herself as an expert
The person presents him/herself as an "independent" scientist or "independent" researcher
The person presents him/herself as a researcher and does not have any reference on researchgate.com or scholar.google.com
The person presents him/herself as a researcher and does not have any article published in one of the main scientific publications listed HERE
The person presents him/herself as a researcher but his/her résumé presents the profile of a practitioner
The person presents him/herself as a researcher but does not come to your event to talk in his/her field
The person wants to talk about health issue(s) but is not a medical doctor
PREPARATION & COACHING
The person often refers to their title (PhD, Doctor, Professor...) to make a point
The candidate does not collaborate with his/her coach to prepare his/her talk
The candidate does not want to write down his/her talk
The candidate does not want to provide his/her slides before the last rehearsal
The candidate does not respect the deadlines or does not attend the last rehearsal
The candidate insists on mentioning his/her company name and/or including the company logo in the slides

A total of 1 or 2 flags is already concerning. It probably means that the person has his/her own agenda to participate and that the coaching will be difficult.

Beyond 3 flags, TEDx is definitely not the right place for the person to give a talk.
